PandoraRipper

Installation

Due to a recent change in how Chrome handles extensions from outside the chrome webstore, installation is a little less straightforward.

  1. Download the extension
  2. Open your extensions management window. You can do this by opening a new tab/window and pasting chrome://chrome/extensions in the url field.
  3. Drag the extension from the download bar to the extensions window.

Usage

To save a song simply Like it.

Click the extension icon to view and download your saved songs

When you first install the extension it may not be able to download as many as the first four songs on any of your pre-existing stations.
